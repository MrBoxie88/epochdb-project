Raid resets are based on a fixed weekly Wednesday 09:00 UTC schedule for 7-day raids, and every 5 days for Onyxia. World boss timers are tracked personally — click Mark as Killed after a kill to start your countdown. World bosses on Project Epoch trigger a zone-wide PvP event — cross-faction groups are disbanded and the zone becomes a battleground 5 minutes before spawn.
